# 11. License: MIT

Status: Accepted

## Context

The package needs a permissive open-source license. The choice was between MIT
and BSD-3.

## Decision

**MIT.** The package name `celestrak` was verified free on pub.dev before first
publish. pub.dev `topics`: `satellite`, `tle`, `omm`, `orbital`, `celestrak`
(pub.dev caps topics at five; the broader `space` tag was dropped to fit).

## Consequences

- **+** Simplest and most common license in the Dart ecosystem; maximal
  adoption.
